About Bestie Clean

Bestie Clean is a UK-based professional cleaning company with over 15 years of experience in delivering exceptional cleaning services across various sectors. Founded in 2008, we've grown from a small local operation to a nationwide service provider, trusted by businesses large and small.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide reliable, thorough cleaning services that exceed expectations. Today, we specialize in after-builders cleaning, commercial contracts, and office maintenance, serving clients throughout the United Kingdom.

Our Commitment

We're committed to more than just cleaning - we're dedicated to creating healthier, more pleasant environments for your employees, customers, and visitors. Our team undergoes continuous training to stay updated with the latest cleaning techniques, safety protocols, and eco-friendly practices.

Core Values

  • Reliability: Our teams arrive on time, fully equipped, and ready to work
  • Integrity: Honest pricing with no hidden costs or surprise charges
